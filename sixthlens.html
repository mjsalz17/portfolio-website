SIXTHLENS

Sixthlens is a responsive website was built in Webflow. It showcases Photographer Matteo Mangano’s beautiful global photography and videography. It incorporates an online shop and social connections for Matteo’s fans. I was the sole designer and developer for the 2020 award-winning site, which was created for a Future Business Leaders (FBLA) website design competition.
VIEW SITE

Highlights

Social & e-commerce

Sixthlens.com not only acts as a portfolio to showcase Matteo's work, but it provides opportunities for fans to connect with him, as well as a shop from which they can purchase his work. These features were introduced to help Matteo increase his fan base and monetize his work.

Responsive & mobile

The site is designed to be responsive down to 300 pixels. This means that customers can view and purchase Mateo's work on any mobile device.
