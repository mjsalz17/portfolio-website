Salzman Consulting

Salzman Consulting is a responsive website built and maintained in Wix. It is a small business consulting site designed to promote experience strategy, research & design services. I work with the stakeholders at Salzman Consulting to design, implement, and maintain this site.

Highlights

Mobile friendly

The site is designed to be responsive down to 300 pixels. Allows users to view both the site and the blog on their mobile devices.

Blog & research

Blog designed to highlight the current research taking place at Salzman Consulting.

Brand awareness

Salzman Consulting has worked with notable Fortune 500 clients. This site is designed to highlight the stellar work that Salzman Consulting has done in the past.
