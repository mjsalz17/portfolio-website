Generate Website

Generate is Northeastern’s student-led Product Development studio. As part of Generate’s design team, I am working with student designers and engineers to redesign and maintain their website. Our goal is to create a newly responsive and engaging site that allows both client-side and student-side applications.
VIEW SITE

Highlights

Offerings

Help design the offerings for Generate. Being a product development studio focusing on hardware & software, we separated the two into different offerings.

Navigation

The site’s navigation is designed to address the unique needs of students who want to work at Generate and clients who would like to request Generate’s hardware or software services. We are currently developing prototypes for these new features.
